How this works
Pick one kill type
A megacharged boss drops far more than a normal one. Mixing them gives meaningless rates — filter to a single type to read real numbers.
Normal kills pool together
Every normal boss shares the same drop profile, so Big Papa and other normal bosses are pooled into one larger, comparable sample.
Sample size matters
Rates need volume to be trustworthy. The coloured pill shows confidence — green is solid, amber is thin, red is too early to trust.
What the numbers mean
Drop rate = share of kills that dropped at least one core. Per-core % = how often that core shows up across all kills. 1/N = roughly one every N kills.
Crowd-sourced & fair
Anyone can log. Logged data can't be edited or deleted by others, junk is rejected automatically, and no single account can skew the totals.
